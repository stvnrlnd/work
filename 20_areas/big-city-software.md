# Big City Software

## What It Is

Big City Software is the SaaS products brand under [[segment-holdings|Segment Holdings]]. It is focused exclusively on software products — no client services work. It operates as an organizational group (not yet a formal subsidiary).

Client work lives under the [[steven-roland|Steven Roland freelance brand]] for now, with the intent to eventually migrate clients here once Big City Software has enough traction as a brand. At that point, client work would be wound down entirely in favor of pure product focus.

## Projects

- [[10_projects/sendfeed.to|sendfeed.to]] — BYOP RSS digest email platform. In development.
- [[10_projects/tokidoki.pro|tokidoki.pro]] — Team operating tool with meeting intelligence. Ideation.

## Mission

Build useful, lean software tools that solve real problems without unnecessary infrastructure complexity. The end goal is a product-only business — no client dependency.
