# Segment Holdings

## What It Is

Segment Holdings is a Delaware LLC formed through Stripe Atlas, owned and operated by Steven Roland. It is registered as a foreign entity in Florida, where Steven lives and works. It is structured as a holding company, intended to eventually own subsidiaries across different business lines. As of 2026, no formal subsidiaries have been incorporated — most projects are operated directly under Segment Holdings, though some (like stevenroland.com) operate under Steven's name personally rather than the LLC.

## People

- **Montanna Allgood** (`montanna@segment.team`) — Steven's wife and collaborator on some projects.

The business is self-funded. No external investment. Survives on funds Steven personally deposits. No products have launched yet and no revenue has been generated.
