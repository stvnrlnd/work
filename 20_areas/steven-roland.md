# Steven Roland (Freelance)

## What It Is

The freelance web development and business consulting practice operating under Steven's name personally — not under Segment Holdings LLC. The brand home is [[10_projects/stevenroland.com|stevenroland.com]].

## Strategy

This is a deliberate, time-limited phase:

1. **Now — build the client list** — freelance under the Steven Roland brand. Take on clients, generate revenue, establish reputation.
2. **Migration phase** — once Big City Software has traction, migrate clients over to it as a brand. Steven Roland becomes a referring/feeder entity.
3. **Exit client work** — stop taking freelance clients entirely. Focus shifts fully to [[big-city-software|Big City Software]]'s SaaS products.

The end goal is a pure software product company. Client work is the bridge to get there.
