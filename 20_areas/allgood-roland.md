# Allgood Roland

## What It Is

Allgood Roland is the personal and family projects group — a shared umbrella for Steven and Montanna's personal sites, their children's projects, and family-oriented work. The name combines Allgood (Montanna's surname) and Roland (Steven's surname).

Not all of these projects are under Segment Holdings LLC. Some operate under Steven's name personally (stevenroland.com), and Montanna's projects are solely hers — if they were ever separated, she would keep her properties or they'd be treated as clients under Steven's freelance practice.
