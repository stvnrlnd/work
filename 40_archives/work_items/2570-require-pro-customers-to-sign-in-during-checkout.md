# 2570-require-pro-customers-to-sign-in-during-checkout

## Description

When the checkout email matches an existing PRO customer account, we should require them to log in so they receive their discount and we don’t need to issue a refund.  
(Currently, users are allowed to dismiss the modal).

We also need to restore the messaging for PRO users to remind them that their login is required to receive their program discounts/benefits.

Please use your best judgment on what and where the dialog box should say to inform the pro users they must log in. We can use some sort of AI tool to generate that comment.
