# 2299-research-send-users-text-updates-for-quotes-order-statuses

## Description

Can we research the ability to send text updates for quotes & order statuses?

We don’t know enough about how this would work with our back end, so we want to start with a research card for you guys to investigate some options and then we can go from there.

---

The cheapest way to send transactional SMS for e-commerce is using API-based providers like Telnyx, [Twilio](https://www.twilio.com/en-us/sms/pricing/us), or ClickSend, which offer competitive pay-as-you-go rates. Using **A2P 10DLC** (Application-to-Person 10-Digit Long Code) ensures high deliverability and lower costs for business messaging.

**Tips for Minimizing Costs**

- **Use A2P 10DLC:** Register your brand and use 10-digit local numbers for the best rates and deliverability in the US.
    
- **Avoid Short Codes:** These are generally more expensive for low-to-medium volume compared to 10DLC.
    
- **Optimize Message Length:** Keep messages under 160 characters to avoid being charged for multiple segments.
