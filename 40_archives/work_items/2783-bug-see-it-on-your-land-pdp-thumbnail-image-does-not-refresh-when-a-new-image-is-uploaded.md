# 2783-bug-see-it-on-your-land-pdp-thumbnail-image-does-not-refresh-when-a-new-image-is-uploaded

## Description

Lexie tried to replace the see it on your land cut-out image for 9401-00109 - we updated the renderings for that plan, so she wanted to use a cut-out of the refreshed rendering. You can see on the landing page that the cut out itself updated: [https://www.houseplans.net/floorplans/940100109/see-it-on-land](https://www.houseplans.net/floorplans/940100109/see-it-on-land) , but the thumbnail image on the PDP is still the original image:

![](https://trello.com/1/cards/69e78b9d44b65e3ce932ed84/attachments/69e78bf6d494f55137f35c57/previews/69e78bf6d494f55137f35c68/download/image.webp "Attachment")

We tried the following things, and still can’t get the new image to show as the thumbnail  
- Uploading an image to replace the old one and saving  
- Using the “delete” button next to the upload feature to delete out the old image, then uploading the new one  
- Using the “delete” button next to the upload feature, unchecking the “show link” button, saving, then going back in and uploading a new image & rechecking the “show link button”  
- Clearing our cache

This is also an issue because she uploaded a cutout without a transparent background by accident on another plan and saved, and went to replace it, but the thumbnail is still showing a plan with a white block around it. For that plan, we just unchecked “show link” until this is resolved.
