Choosing a house plan often starts with location. Building codes, climate conditions, lot sizes and regional preferences all influence which home designs are most popular in a given state. Our Top Plans by State collection highlights house plans that are frequently searched, selected and built across each state.

This curated resource makes it easy to explore house plans by location while still showcasing today's most in-demand styles, including Modern Farmhouse, Barndominium, Craftsman, Ranch and Traditional designs. Each state collection features plans that are adaptable to local climates, building requirements and lifestyle needs.

Whether you are building in a coastal region, a suburban neighborhood, open countryside or a colder climate zone, our state-based collections help you quickly find proven designs that builders and homeowners return to again and again. Every plan can be customized to better match your lot, code requirements or personal preferences.
