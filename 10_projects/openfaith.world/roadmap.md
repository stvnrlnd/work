# openfaith.world — Roadmap

## Current State

The site is live. It is a statically generated Statamic site hosted on Cloudflare Pages. A nightly GitHub Action publishes scheduled posts.

No monetization is active yet.

---

## Blockers

- **[[sendfeed.to]]** — The newsletter will be sent via sendfeed.to, which isn't finished yet. Phase 1 can't start until sendfeed.to's core digest loop is built and production-ready.

---

## Open Questions

- **Content naming** — sermon-style pieces need a name that fits the non-religious framing. "Sermon" is the working label but isn't right for this context.
- **Sponsor perks** — premium newsletter, content pack discount, and site badge are the current thinking, but specifics (badge design, discount amount, etc.) are TBD.
- **Content pack pricing** — no fixed price established yet.
- **Newsletter platform** — not documented; needs to be decided or confirmed.

## Content Pipeline (Ongoing)

Once the first content pack ships, the pipeline is:

1. **New pack** — sold near end of year for the coming year
2. **Prior pack** — pieces roll into the weekly premium newsletter over the following year
3. **Sponsors** — receive premium newsletter content as part of their perks
