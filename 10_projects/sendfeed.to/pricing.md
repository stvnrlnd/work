# sendfeed.to — Pricing

## Tiers

| Tier | Price | Feeds | Subscribers |
|------|-------|-------|-------------|
| Starter | $6/month | 1 | 1,000 |
| Growth | $14/month | 5 | 10,000 |
| Pro | $29/month | Unlimited | 50,000 |

Feed limits are per account, not per project. Subscribers limits are also per account, across all projects.

Additional subscribers are available on any tier at **$1 per 1,000**.

## Subscriber Overflow

Subscribers can sign up for a project even after a plan's subscriber limit is reached. However, those subscribers will not receive digests and cannot be managed until the user either purchases additional subscriber blocks or upgrades their plan.

This is intentional — it avoids turning away subscribers at the point of signup while still incentivizing the user to pay for growth.
