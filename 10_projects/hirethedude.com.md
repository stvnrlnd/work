# hirethedude.com — Overview

## What It Is

hirethedude.com is a client site for a local maintenance man who has formed his own independent business while maintaining full-time employment at an apartment complex. The site serves as both a marketing presence and a functional tool for prospective clients to learn about his services and get in touch or book him.

## Client

Montanna's step-father. First official client engagement under the Steven Roland freelance brand. If/when clients migrate to Big City Software, this would move under that brand.

## Goals

- Establish an online presence for the business
- Build trust with prospective clients (residential and small commercial)
- Provide a simple path to booking or contacting him

## Core Features

- **Service listing** — What he offers (general maintenance, handyman work, etc.)
- **Contact form** — Simple way for prospective clients to reach out
- **Booking tool** — Mechanism for clients to schedule work (form-based or integrated booking flow; details TBD)
- **About / credibility** — Who he is, his background, why hire him

## Status

Pre-development. No code written yet. Docs being initialized.
