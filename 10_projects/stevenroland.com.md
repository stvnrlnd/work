# stevenroland.com

## What It Is

Steven's personal website. It serves three purposes:

1. **Marketing site** — freelance web development and business consulting services offered under Steven's name directly (not [[20_areas/segment-holdings|Segment Holdings LLC]])
2. **Living resume** — professional history, skills, and work samples
3. **Blog** — writing on development, business, and whatever else

## Services Offered

- Laravel / PHP web application development
- Business consulting (strategy, operations, systems)
- Website builds for small businesses

## Target Audience

Small businesses and individuals in need of custom web development or business consulting — particularly clients who value working directly with a senior developer rather than an agency.

## Strategic Role

stevenroland.com is a deliberate, time-limited revenue bridge:

1. **Now** — generate freelance and consulting income under the Steven Roland brand
2. **Later** — as [[10_projects/firstcitysoftware.com|First City Software]] gains traction, migrate clients there; stevenroland.com becomes a feeder
3. **Exit** — eventually stop taking clients entirely; focus exclusively on [[20_areas/segment-holdings|Segment Holdings]] SaaS products

Client work is not the destination — it funds the path to a pure product company.

## Notes

- Operates under Steven Roland personally, not [[20_areas/segment-holdings|Segment Holdings LLC]]
- Distinguishes the personal freelance/consulting practice from the LLC's product businesses
